Agnes is [[Bill Maiden]]'s partner. She is 41 years old, originally from Hungary, and has lived in Sydney since 2012. She and Bill have been together since 2016.

## Background

Agnes moved to Sydney from Hungary in 2012. Her relationship with Bill began in early 2016, while Bill was still married to Philippa. The affair was discovered by Philippa in April 2016.

Agnes has been a constant presence through Bill's most difficult period — addiction, rehab, custody battles, and financial volatility.

## Household

Agnes and Bill share a dog, Balazs — an 8-year-old Cavoodle.
