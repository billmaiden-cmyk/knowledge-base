The Maiden Family Superannuation Fund (MFSF) is [[Bill Maiden]]'s Self Managed Superannuation Fund (SMSF), established circa 2020 and administered by [[Altus Financial]]. Its sole asset is shares in Connected Medical Solutions Ltd (trading as My Emergency Doctor). The fund is currently in a qualified audit position due to the illiquid and unlisted nature of that investment.

## Administration

Managed by [[Altus Financial]] (Jeremy D'Cruz, Mariia Avdeeva, Stewart Mortlock). Annual financial statements and tax returns are prepared by Altus and signed via DocuSign. The fund is subject to mandatory annual audit.

## Asset Holdings

The fund's sole investment is shares in Connected Medical Solutions Ltd (My Emergency Doctor). As of the 2025 audit, the auditor issued a qualified opinion noting concerns about recoverability, market value, and liquidity of this unlisted investment, and flagged a potential breach of Section 130 of the Superannuation Industry (Supervision) Act. *(source: Altus 2025 Summary Pack, 2026-03-27)*

## Contribution History

- February 2022: ANZ Staff Super balance rolled in — first transaction in the fund
- June 2023: Employer (My Emergency Doctor) paid three years of outstanding super contributions in one month — triggered excess contributions issue
- Excess concessional contributions tax assessed by ATO: ~$21k (as at April 2025); Altus managing election options with ATO

## PAYG Instalments

The ATO levies quarterly PAYG instalments of $2,124 against the fund (as of 2025–26). Bill queried this given no contributions and a potentially impaired sole asset; Altus is reviewing whether instalments remain necessary. *(Updated 2026-04-20, Gmail search)*
