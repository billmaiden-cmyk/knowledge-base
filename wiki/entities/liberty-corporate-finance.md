Liberty Corporate Finance is an Australian corporate finance advisory firm in which [[Bill Maiden]] holds a 16% equity stake. It is described in Bill's generational wealth plan as the highest return-on-Bill's-time asset in his portfolio — where his PE judgment and strategic relationships convert most directly into value creation.

## Bill's Position

| Item | Value |
|---|---|
| Bill's actual cash cost base | ~$100k |
| Bill's equity stake | 16% |
| Profit share (Years 1–2) | 12% (half of 24% total pool) |
| Profit share (Year 3+) | 8% (one-third of 24% pool, 3 partners) |
| Bill's base salary | $400k/year |

The $100k actual cost base is critical. The position's intrinsic value is far higher; Bill's personal return on invested capital is calculated against $100k, not the fair market value of the stake.

## Operating Model

**Years 1–2:** Two delivery partners (Tom and Bill), each capable of up to 12 deals/year. Total capacity: 24 deals at ~$600k/deal.

**Year 3+:** A third partner added at $400k base salary, adding ~12 deals/year capacity. The profit share pool stays at 24% total but splits three ways (8% each).

**Cost structure:** Partner base salaries + 30% of revenue as corporate operating expenses (office, staff, technology, PI insurance, travel, BD).

## What Must Be True

1. Tom must build the deal pipeline from 6 toward 28–36 deals/year
2. Fee levels must hold at ~$600k as volume grows
3. The APAC market must support Cooper Parry–level multiples at exit
4. Bill's 16% must not be diluted through the growth phase
5. The 3rd partner (Year 3) must contribute meaningfully to capacity
6. Bill must invest meaningful strategic time — this is where his hour is most valuable

## Strategic Importance

The wealth plan explicitly states that even though Bill owns 100% of MTS and only 16% of Liberty, *more of his personal strategic time should go to Liberty*. Liberty scales with far less friction than strata, and Bill does not hate the work. The biggest risk to Liberty is Bill getting re-absorbed into MTS operations — which is why the [[More Than Strata (MTS)]] GM hire is a precondition for Liberty reaching its potential.
