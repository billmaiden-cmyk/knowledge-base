### EML Payments (ASX: EML)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Company** | EML Payments Ltd |
| **ASX code** | EML |
| **Sector** | Fintech / Payments |
| **Contact** | Anthony Hynes — CEO |
| **Status** | Prospect — early stage |
| **Source** | Bill Maiden, 14 Apr 2026 |
| **Identified** | 2026-04-14 |

**Situation:** EML Payments is an ASX-listed global payments and fintech company. The share price has come off materially from prior highs. PE interest has been noted. The depressed valuation and PE activity make this a potential advisory or transaction opportunity for Liberty CF.

**Context:** EML has faced regulatory headwinds including a protracted engagement with the Central Bank of Ireland over its PFS Card Services Ireland subsidiary (~2022–2024). This drove significant de-rating. With those issues partially resolved or behind it, the stock remains well below peak — a classic distressed-valuation PE entry window.

**Bill's thesis:** Price dislocation + PE interest = potential transaction. Intel gathering stage. No outreach made yet.

**Antonella/Ron intel (14 Apr):** Ron thinks EML will get PE calls. Antonella: *"I said let's take it private."* Anthony reportedly hates PE — *"rather talk to us listed guys than deal with punks that tell him what to do"* — but right deal/right price could change that. Antonella: *"I like him a lot, if I had the money I would back him and give him a long rope."* Bill: *"He might make more money with PE."* *(source: whatsapp 14 Apr 2026)*

**Angle for Liberty:** CEO-side advisory. Position Liberty as the alternative to traditional PE — help Anthony structure a deal where he retains control/upside. BAP (see below) could be a model for CEO incentive deal structure.

**Next step:** Bill to call Anthony Hynes directly. Lead with *"I reckon you're getting PE calls — we can help you get the best deal."* *(source: whatsapp 14 Apr 2026)*
