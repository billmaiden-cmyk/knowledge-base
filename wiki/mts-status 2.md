## What Moves These Lights

Updated automatically by: `/briefing`, `/midday-pulse`, `/close`, `/ingest-slack`, `/triage`

**Team 🟢** — all direct reports operational, no unresolved personnel issues  
**Team 🟡** — staff transition, performance concern, or capacity issue Keira is managing  
**Team 🔴** — resignation, escalation, or HR matter needing Bill  

**Compliance 🟢** — no open s184, insurance, or licensing items beyond routine  
**Compliance 🟡** — open items being managed; nothing overdue by more than 7 days  
**Compliance 🔴** — overdue compliance item, court matter, or licensing risk  

**Billing 🟢** — trust account operational, levy collections normal, no overdue client payments  
**Billing 🟡** — trust account transition or minor collection issue; Vy/Keira managing  
**Billing 🔴** — trust account breach risk, significant arrears, or payment default  

**Operations 🟢** — Zendesk normal, no escalations, tech running  
**Operations 🟡** — escalated ticket, tech issue, or supplier problem being managed  
**Operations 🔴** — system outage, client escalation requiring Bill, or domain/infrastructure failure  

**Growth 🟢** — pipeline active, GTM executing, Khoi engaged  
**Growth 🟡** — pipeline slow, GTM item stalled, or Khoi not yet ramped  
**Growth 🔴** — GTM blocked, key lead lost, or critical growth dependency at risk
