This is the integrated reference for MTS's business strategy. The **One Page Plan 2026** (March 2026) is the current operating authority. Supplementary context from earlier documents is preserved and clearly marked.

---

## Mission

> Helping communities achieve their goals by managing buildings like (micro) businesses: better advice, improved transparency and faster progress.

*(source: One Page Plan 2026)*

## Vision

> Building Australia's most responsive, outcomes-driven and AI-enabled independent strata platform. Win by giving committees better advice, tangible progress and faster execution, while building the most scalable and profitable operating model in the sector. Use that operating advantage to compound growth organically and through selective acquisition of underperforming books. Repurpose internal capability tooling to build a "My Strata Manager" agent/financial/workflow platform for non-client Committees and OCs.

*(source: One Page Plan 2026)*

**"My Strata Manager" platform vision:** A second-order product play — repurposing internal capability tooling into a SaaS/agent/financial/workflow platform for non-client Committees and OCs. This extends MTS beyond managed services into a platform business.

## Founding Principles

*(source: Organisation Structure_20250701.pdf — Bill's founding philosophy)*

These are the design convictions underneath the strategy. The One Page Plan assumes them; this is where they're stated explicitly:

- Manage buildings like they're a business (OC = company; Strata Committee = Board)
- Service level is about demand and supply — if capacity doesn't match work volume, you always fall behind
- Support top-of-scope to deliver value — most experienced people do most complex work
- Not generalists; be specialists
- Coordinate to have a single view of truth (Zendesk as the operational system of record)
- Responsiveness: acknowledge and action
- Outcomes: get a plan, in control, deliver progress

## Why MTS Will Win — Competitive Advantages

*(source: One Page Plan 2026)*

- **Independent position** — no conflicts of interest with suppliers; already resonating with prospects
- **Proven responsiveness** — first reply, resolution, and backlog metrics already demonstrated
- **Specialist execution model** — replacing generalist strata managers with specialist teams
- **Centralised Zendesk-led visibility** — triage and control across all work (vs email-based competitors)
- **AI and workflow capability** — embedded into operations, reporting, and growth
- **Scalable platform** — increases lots per operator and lowers cost-to-serve

### Value Propositions

*(source: MTS Business Strategy.docx — supplementary)*

Two crisply stated competitive positioning statements from earlier strategic work:

1. **Responsiveness at scale** — capacity matched to client work; full ticket visibility so nothing is lost, everything measured
2. **Expert delivery** — specialists not generalists; team structured around the task

## Three Strategic Pillars

### 1. Better Product

**Goal:** Improve what clients experience.

- Improve owner access (reception)
- Improve Committee visibility, activity, progress and outcomes
- Make MTS easier to work with

**Key initiatives:**
- Inhouse Office HQ → VN Ops: one-touch resolution for simple queries
- AI Voice Reception
- 3-page Managing Agency Agreement (simplified onboarding)
- Committee / Building Report (MVP covering Governance, Insurance, R&M, Fire, Financials)
- Chat with the Committee Report
- Chat with my OC AI Agent
- Chat with my Levy Position Agent

### 2. Better Business

**Goal:** Improve how MTS operates internally — scale lots without linear headcount growth.

- Centralise work in Zendesk with triage, visibility and control
- Design roles around specialist teams and top-of-scope work (RASCI framework)
- Build an AI-enabled operating platform: substrate, orchestration and management intelligence
- Increase lots per FTE and gross margin

**Key initiatives:**
- Operating Substrate / World Model (ongoing — dependency for Committee Report)
- ZD Opportunity Assessment / Roadmap: queues, automations, visibility and reporting
- Specialist team role definition, RASCI and workflow control/ownership
- ZD/AI Renovations Process (end-to-end: ZD form, macros, AI chatbot)
- ZD/AI Keys/Fobs/Access Process
- ZD/AI Audit Trigger
- AI AGM Notice Checker
- **ZD/AI Repairs and Maintenance Agent suite:**
  - Issue Diagnosis Agent
  - Quote Scoping Agent
  - Quote Request Agent
  - Quote Follow-up (ZD)
  - Quote Checker
  - Quote Comparison Agent
  - Quote Approval Request Agent
- Orchestration layer for agent coordination
- AI Exec Team / management cockpit for visibility and decision support

### 3. Stronger Growth

**Goal:** Build a repeatable acquisition and onboarding engine.

- Improve conversion rates on SEM (Google Ads)
- Leverage existing lead database to generate fresh opportunities
- Manage pricing to improve lot profit
- Use growth operations and automation to scale BD

**Key initiatives:**
- Recruit VN Growth Ops Specialist (Vietnam-based, lower-cost high-volume outbound role — not a senior Australian BD hire)
- Lead Database Campaign (develop compelling offer, launch with Growth Spec)
- Build target list, outbound and follow-up engine
- Develop proposal, proof asset and case study pack
- Pipeline reporting and growth visibility
- Progressive website improvements

## Mission/Vision Evolution

The mission and vision language sharpened significantly between planning cycles:

| | Earlier (Business Strategy.docx) | Current (One Page Plan 2026) |
|---|---|---|
| **Mission** | Deliver clients an unrivalled level of responsive service and expertise to improve the experience and outcomes for Strata Committee and Owners | Helping communities achieve their goals by managing buildings like (micro) businesses: better advice, improved transparency and faster progress |
| **Vision** | More Than Strata is considered the leading strata management business in Australia achieving rapid growth from a superior product made possible by experts enabled by AI | Building Australia's most responsive, outcomes-driven and AI-enabled independent strata platform... *(full text in Vision section above)* |

The shift: from aspirational/internal ("considered the leading...") to operational and customer-centred ("helping communities achieve their goals"). The 2026 vision also introduces the platform play ("My Strata Manager") and acquisition strategy explicitly.
