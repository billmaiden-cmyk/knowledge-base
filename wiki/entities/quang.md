Quang is Technology Lead at [[More Than Strata (MTS)]], responsible for both hands-on development and leadership of the technology team. He leads a small team of Bach (Developer) and Andrew (IT Administrator). Previous tech team members (Alex, Cerris, Ngoc) have all departed. Quang is one of [[Bill Maiden]]'s five direct reports.

## Role & Accountabilities

**Role type:** Technology Lead (Developer + Team Leader)

**Purpose:** Deliver and maintain MTS's technology platform — the applications, integrations, and infrastructure that underpin the specialist team model and AI-enabled operations.

**Key accountabilities:**
- Core platform stability: Zendesk, StrataMax, PropertyIQ, Box
- Development and deployment of new workflows and integrations
- Technical delivery of [[MTS Operating Substrate (World Model)]] initiatives

- Infrastructure and security (Azure-hosted)
- Support for AI workflow development (in coordination with Bill/external)
