Keira holds the role of **Business Systems & Operations Lead (Strata OS)** at MTS — effectively a Chief of Staff / Operating System role designed to reduce Bill's bottleneck, translate strata operations into buildable AI workflows, and oversee Vietnam operations delivery. The role is structured around three pillars and is explicitly scoped to keep Keira high-leverage (not an executor of routine tasks). *(source: raw/Keira Role.docx)*

**Internal tagline:** "Keira runs our business operating system: priorities, process design, and Vietnam delivery so we execute without Bill as the router."

---

## Three Pillars

### Pillar 1 — Executive Operations & CEO Leverage (Chief of Staff style)

**Objective:** Reduce Bill bottlenecking by owning the decision-to-execution loop and making priorities stick.

**Key responsibilities:**
- **Decision funnel** — collect inputs, filter noise, bring Bill only decisions that truly need him (with options + recommendation)
- **Execution rhythm** — run the weekly priorities cadence (Top 5 outcomes, owners, deadlines, blockers)
- **Follow-through** — maintain the action register and chase closure so commitments don't evaporate
- **Pre-briefs** — one-page briefs for key meetings/calls (context, decision required, recommended approach)
- **Escalation triage** — handle most escalations; only elevate when it genuinely needs Bill

**Outputs:**
- Weekly "Top 5 outcomes" doc + owners + deadlines
- Decisions log + action register
- Brief pack for key meetings

**Success indicators:**
- Bill gets fewer ad-hoc pings and context dumps
- Priorities don't churn mid-week
- More issues resolved without Bill as coordinator

---

### Pillar 2 — Strata OS Product Translation & Adoption

**Objective:** Turn strata operational reality into buildable, testable workflows and ensure adoption so automation sticks.

**Key responsibilities:**
- **Workflow mapping** — document real end-to-end processes for priority domains (fire compliance, committee reporting, repairs, approvals, levy/arrears)
- **Requirements/specs** — write clear briefs for the Engineering Manager: inputs/outputs, triggers, roles, exceptions, data fields, dependencies
- **Acceptance criteria** — define "done" operationally; prevent "built but unusable"
- **Standardisation before automation** — SOPs, checklists, templates, Zendesk macros to reduce variance and rework
- **Rollout & change management** — pilots, training, comms, feedback loops, metrics; iterate until embedded

**Outputs:**
- Process maps (as-is + to-be)
- PRD-lite briefs with acceptance criteria
- SOP + template library linked to workflows
- Rollout plan per workflow (pilot → scale)

**Success indicators:**
- Faster cycle time: idea → deployed workflow
- Less rework between ops and build
- Higher adoption and fewer edge-case failures

**Important interface:** Keira owns "what/why + adoption." Engineering Manager owns "how + technical delivery."

---

### Pillar 3 — Vietnam Operations Oversight & Delivery

**Objective:** Make Vietnam operations a predictable, accountable delivery engine that supports Sydney service quality and Strata OS execution.

**Key responsibilities:**
- **Team oversight** — day-to-day management of Vietnam ops function (work allocation, standards, performance, coaching, coverage)
- **Delivery cadence** — daily/weekly ops rhythm focused on throughput, quality, and SLA performance (not engineering sprints)
- **Quality control** — enforce checklists, documentation standards, ticket hygiene, QA before work is "done"
- **Operational reporting** — weekly reporting on volumes, backlog ageing, SLA performance, rework, recurring pain points
- **Cross-team coordination** — smooth handoffs between Sydney and Vietnam; reduce "lost in translation" failures
- **Continuous improvement loop** — identify repeat issues and feed into Pillar 2 (SOP/template/automation backlog)
- **Hiring/onboarding** — help recruit and train Vietnam ops roles; ensure playbooks exist

**Outputs:**
- Vietnam ops daily standup notes + blocker list
- Weekly ops performance snapshot (volumes, backlog, SLA, rework)
- Ticket hygiene and QA standards
- Training playbooks for Vietnam roles + onboarding checklist

**Success indicators:**
- Stable throughput and predictable turnaround times
- Cleaner tickets, fewer bounced requests, less rework
- Sydney team experiences fewer interruptions and escalations
- Bill doesn't need to intervene to make Vietnam move

---

## Decision Rights

Keira has authority to:
- Set and run the weekly priorities cadence; require owners/dates
- Enforce that work is "real" only when it has owner + due date + definition of done
- Gate operational requirements into Strata OS build queue via standard brief + acceptance criteria
- Approve/hold operational rollout based on readiness + adoption criteria
- Set Vietnam ops standards for ticket hygiene, QA, turnaround, and escalate performance issues

---

## Interfaces

| Counterpart | How Keira works with them |
|-------------|--------------------------|
| **Bill** | Keira brings decisions, tradeoffs, and escalation only when needed. Bill sets direction and makes high-leverage calls. |
| **Engineering Manager** | Keira provides workflow briefs + acceptance criteria + rollout needs. EM delivers solution design and build. |
| **Sydney Ops / Team Leads** | Ops owns execution. Keira owns system design, standardisation, and adoption. Ops escalates friction to Keira. |
| **Vietnam Ops Team** | Keira sets cadence, standards, reporting, coaching, and ensures predictable delivery. |

---

## KPIs

| Area | Metric |
|------|--------|
| CEO leverage | Reduction in Bill interruptions; % actions closed on time from weekly priority list |
| Strata OS adoption | Cycle time: workflow identified → rolled out; adoption rate of new SOPs/macros/workflows; reduction in repeat escalations |
| Vietnam ops | SLA / turnaround time by ticket type; backlog ageing; rework / bounced ticket rate |

---

## Boundaries

- **Keira is not** the day-to-day executor of service workflows (e.g., fire compliance tickets)
- She operates at systems + oversight + exceptions/audits; routine execution owned by backfill/ops teams
- **Keira does not** manage engineering delivery — only requirements/adoption and Vietnam ops delivery
