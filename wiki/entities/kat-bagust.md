Kathryn (Kat) Bagust is Operations Manager at [[More Than Strata (MTS)]]. She leads triage, overwatch, and the specialist teams for repairs & maintenance, insurance, and SP/client data. Kat is the operational pulse of the business — she sees every ticket and ensures service levels hold. She is one of [[Bill Maiden]]'s five direct reports.

## Role & Accountabilities

**Role type:** Operations Manager + Specialist Team Lead *(source: raw/Organisation Structure_20250701.pdf)*

**Purpose:** Coordinate Specialist Teams with Senior Strata Managers so clients experience responsive service and achieve desired outcomes in timely ways.

**Key accountabilities:**
- Triage inbound work to the right resource at the right time with appropriate due dates
- Overwatch portfolio service responsiveness — target 4-hour first reply median
- Coach team members on responsiveness and Zendesk usage
- Lead R&M specialist team: rapid turnaround, short-sprint cadence work
- Lead Insurance specialist team: renewals and claims
- Lead SP/Client Data maintenance (supported by Wins)

**Key outcomes (from org structure):**
- Specialist Teams fulfil tasks in organised ways with visibility of progress
- Service levels within target ranges
- Team members are effective users of Zendesk
- Specialist function requirements met to due dates and standards
- First reply targets met
