## Summary

Modra Technology is a Warragul (Victoria, Australia) robotics and creel manufacturing business serving the global tufted carpet industry. ABGF holds 40% for $10–15m on a $35m valuation. Founded by Tim Modra. CEO Craig Fuller (8 months in role as of Apr 26). Chair Michael Morley. Board observer Elliot Foote (ABGF). Board of 5 plus CFO and observer. CFO Asraar Waliuddin.

The flagship product is **CreelMT** — a robotic creel automation system for carpet tufting machines. Sales cycle 18-24 months; ASP ~AUD $2.4m per system; 50% gross margin; 30% EBITDA at budget run-rate. Customers include Tarkett (USA), Interface (USA + Australia), Mohawk, Shaw, Engineered Floors / J&J, Milliken, Burmatex, Modulyss. Distribution via agents in Japan/Korea/Europe/Belgium/USA/UK/Türkiye/India.

[[Bill Maiden]] is being onboarded as ABGF Portfolio Director with first board meeting Tuesday 28 April 2026 at ABGF Melbourne (Board Meeting No 18).

## Industry & Product

- **CreelMT**: Robotic creel automation for carpet tufting machines. Reduces yarn handling labour, increases machine uptime. ~380 build hours per system (stabilised). ~$2.4m AUD ASP. Sales cycle 18-24 months. Tech Level 1 (qualified) leads convert at probabilities — 40-60 leads needed in pipeline at any time to deliver 15-18 systems pa.
- **Sampling machines**: Mtuft, Kibby, Hollow Needle. ASP ~$200-450k USD. Lower margin contributor.
- **Spare parts & service**: Steady revenue stream; Milliken USA in negotiation for first standalone maintenance contract (USD 165k pa = ~$250k AUD).
- **Fixed Creel** (in development): Stage Gate 1 (Discovery/Ideation). Automated mobile robot (AMR) based, automated splicing. Critical strategic project — Modra's next product. Patent strategy staged (file when design stable).
