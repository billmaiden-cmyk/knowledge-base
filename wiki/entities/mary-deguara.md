**LIC obligations (delegated from Bill, April 2026):**

## Management Model: Exception-Based Governance

Mary operates as the **sign-off layer**, not the executor. She does not chase compliance tasks — she reviews exception reports and acts only when something is red.

| Area | Who does the work | What Mary sees | Mary acts when |
|---|---|---|---|
| AGM deadlines | Wins/Thao prep | Exception report: overdue or <30 days with no draft | Red flag |
| Insurance currency | [[Kat Bagust]] manages renewals | Exception report: expired or <30 days out | Red flag |
| Fire/AFSS | [[Matt Wrigley]] manages compliance | Exception report: overdue AFSS or open fire orders | Red flag |
| Payment approvals | Daily queue | Approval queue — direct action | Daily (LIC obligation) |
| Trust reconciliation | Gino/Leo execute | Monthly exception: unreconciled accounts | Red flag |
| CPD | Team members complete | Exception: anyone overdue | Red flag |

**Key principle:** The metric is not "ensure AGMs are done" — it's "no exceptions unaddressed within 48 hours." *(Updated 2026-04-11 ~10:00 AEST, CEO stack build session)*
